North America

is entirely in the northern hemisphere and consist of two countries. Around 579 million people live on this continent.

Canada is one of the countries on this continent.

South America

is mostly in the Sourthen Hemisphere including 12 countries, and 2 dependants. An average of 433 million people live on the continent.

Brazil is one of the countries on this continent.

Africa

is mostly located in the Eastern hemisphere and consists of 54 countries. Around 1.2 billion people live on this continent.

Egypt and Equatorial Guinea are countries on this continent.

Europe

is a continent located entirely in the Northern Hemisphere and mainly in the Eastern Hemisphere. Europe is known as the mother of humanity, for the contribution of its countries to the modern world.

France is one of the countries on this continent.

Asia

South Korea is located in East Asia. It sits on the Korean peninsula which borders China. Its current population is around 51 million people.

South Korea is one of the countries on this continent.

Oceania

is located in the Eastern and Western hemisphere, Oceania is home to 14 countries. It is also home for at least 41 million people

Fiji is one of the countries on this continent.
