South Korea

The Republic of Korea is located in East Asia and sits on the southern half of the Korean Peninsula. It shares a land border with North Korea.

Cultural Facts

South Korea is one of the most popular tourist destinations in East Asia. It is a country that is deeply rooted in its traditions and customs. Family is incredibly important in Korean culture and they follow an age hierchy. Religion is practiced freely, with Christianity and Buddhism being two of the most practiced religions there.

Languages

Korean is the official language of South Korea. South Korea is one of the most homogenous countries in the world

Food

Korean food is largely based on rice, vegetables, and meat. Kimchi is the country's national dish, it is a variety of vegetables which are fermented and can be stored for a long time. One of the most popular Korean meat dishes is Bulgogi, a savory dishes of grilled marinated beef.

Tourist Hotspots

  • Seoul, South Korea's capital
  • Gyeongbokgung Palace
  • Namsan Seoul Tower
  • Lotte World
  • Namiseom Island
  • Insa-dong

Average Cost to Travel

The average daily cost for one person to travel to a city in South Korea is $99. A one month visit for one person be roughly $3,000. This price would differ based on whether you're traveling to a popular city or the countryside. However, there are many ways to budget on your trip. Street food is a lot cheaper than conventional restaurants and there are a lot of activities that are free.

  • Avg Cost of One Week Trip to Seoul for 1 person: $719
  • Budget Cost of One Week Trip to Seoul for 1 person: $275
  • Avg Cost of Hotel in Seoul for 1 person: $52
  • Bugdet Cost of Hotel in Seoul for 1 person: $20

Current Events

  • Current President of South Korea is Moon Jae-in
  • South Korea had a fast response to the COVID-19 pandemic and as such was one of the first countries to control the spread and recover.
  • More Westerners are being exposed to Korean culture through entertainers like BTS and director Park Boon Joon-ho who directed the academy award winning film, Parasite.
