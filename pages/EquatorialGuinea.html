Cutural facts

Equatorial Guinea, also known as EG, is a small country located in the heart of Africa. Its capital is Malabo.

EG has many different cultural facts, such as ethnic groups, languages, traditional food, and holidays. Some of its traditional dances are Djebol, Ekoo, and Bonko, that I advise you to watch or dance while enjoying a good time in this small country.

You can visit the cultural centers (Centro Cultural Equatoguineano or the Centro Cultural EspÃ±ol) to know more about its culture and history.

Current events

Classification of the men's soccer team for CAN 2021.

The EG men's soccer team, known as "Nzalng Masculino"; defeated the Tanzanian men's soccer team on March 25, in the " Estadio Malabo" which allowed the EG team to classify for the African Cup of Nations 2021.

Military base explosion.

On March, 7 an explosion took place in one of the military bases in Bata, the economic capital. It was the first time that this kind of event happens in this country.
